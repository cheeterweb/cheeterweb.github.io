Who are we?

Cheeter is an Indie Game Studio which makes with the Unity Game Engine 2D and 3D Games. Cheeter was originally "founded"
(We dont have a real Company or smt this is just a for fun project) in 2021 by me (Kuki), and Jojo (His nickname). It was originally a Site to get tips for Videogames.
but 3 Months after Jojo left bc he just wasnt feeling it anymore. Then we changed, alot. First was it the Videogame site, then a Healthy & Lifestyle Page, then a Computer Forum and then
(finally) it was what it is now, a Videogame & Web Publisher. We are trying to make fun & productive experiences for everyone to enjoy.
